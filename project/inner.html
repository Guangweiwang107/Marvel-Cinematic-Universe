The Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU)

Walt Disney and Marvel

Marvel Comics (Marvel Comics) and Detective Comics (DC Comics) are the two major comics companies in the United States. It was founded in 1939 and officially named Marvel in 1961, formerly translated as "Marvel Comics". It is Timely; Atlas Comics.

In April 1939, the company created the world's first mutant anti-hero, Namor, in the inaugural issue of Movie Comic Weekly. Mackenzie, MARVEL's first superhero, predated the company's founding by six months. In October of that year, the company created the world's first cyborg superhero, the Original Thunderfire, in the first issue of Marvel Comics, and introduced Namor.

Mackenzie brought it in, and this combination of fire and water delivered the first punch in MARVEL history. In late 2009, MARVEL was acquired by the Walt Disney Company for $4.24 billion and became a subsidiary.
